After paying the entrance fee, you will be able to walk around in the bugCatching Zone. To encounter Pokémon, you must either walk in the tall grass or walk into Pokémon that randomly appear on the map.

When you have encountered a Pokémon, you will be given 4 options:

  • Ball: This will throw a Poké Ball and give you a chance of capturing the Pokémon.
  • Rock: Throwing a rock will make the Pokémon more likely to flee, but easier to catch.
  • Bait: Throwing bait will make the Pokémon less likely to flee, but harder to catch. If you have Razz or Nanab berries, you can use them as bait for special effects.
  • Run: This will end the encounter.

After your turn of the encounter, if it did not result in the encounter ending, the wild Pokémon will have a chance of running away.

Your bugCatching exploration will end when you have run out of Poké Balls. You can freely leave and re-enter without having to pay the fee again as long as you still have Poké Balls remaining.
